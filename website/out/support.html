Frequently Asked Questions

How do I integrate CancelKit with my website?

Integrating CancelKit is simple. After signing up, you'll receive a JavaScript snippet to add to your website. For detailed instructions, check out our onboarding wizard or documentation.

Which payment processors does CancelKit support?

CancelKit currently supports Stripe, PayPal, Paddle, Chargebee, and Recurly. We're constantly adding more integrations. If you use a different payment processor, please contact us.

Is CancelKit compliant with the FTC's Click to Cancel rule?

Yes, CancelKit is specifically designed to help businesses comply with the FTC's Click to Cancel rule. Our solution makes it as easy for customers to cancel as it was to sign up, as required by the regulations.

Can I customize the cancellation button and modal?

Absolutely! You can customize the button text, colors, and placement. Premium plans also allow for full branding customization of the cancellation modal.

How does CancelKit handle cancellation reporting?

CancelKit automatically generates monthly compliance reports that document all cancellation requests and their outcomes. These reports are stored securely and can be accessed through your dashboard.
